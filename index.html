Common Questions

Everything you need to know about HomeConnect.

Are the properties verified?

Yes, every property listed on HomeConnect undergoes a rigorous verification process by our team to ensure the details and images are accurate.

How do I book an inspection?

Once you find a property you like, simply click "Book Inspection" on the property details page. You can pay the inspection fee securely via Paystack.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely. We use Paystack, a leading payment processor, to handle all transactions. HomeConnect never stores your card details.

Can I list my own property?

Yes! Register as an Agent in the app to start listing properties and reaching thousands of students looking for a home.
